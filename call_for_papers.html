We welcome researchers in the field to submit papers to be presented as posters. Submitted manuscripts should be up to 2 pages plus references, formatted according to ICRA standards using the Paper Template downloadable on the IEEE ICRA 2021 website (two-column format). We encourage authors to submit a video clip to complement their manuscript. Submissions will be selected based on their originality, relevance to the workshop topics, contributions, technical clarity, and presentation.

To submit your contributions please follow: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=lhmp2021

Submission deadline: 25th April 2021 (extended), 23:59 CEST (1:59 PM PDT)
Notifications: May 15, 2021
